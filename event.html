The Aladdin's Pizza
Journey
Today

A beloved Midlands brand known for quality pizzas, community spirit, and unbeatable flavour.

2021

Moved to a larger, modern store, reflecting strong customer loyalty and growth.

2023

Expanded to Willenhall, marking the start of Aladdin’s regional journey

2024

Opened new stores in Great Barr, Rugeley, Wolverhampton, Brownhills, and Cannock

At Aladdin’s, every pizza is crafted with a purpose - to bring people together through unbeatable flavour and quality. The journey began in 2012 in Pizza Takeaway in Wednesbury, where the very first takeaway store opened. With a passion for fresh ingredients and a vision to create the best local pizza near me delivery service, Aladdin’s quickly became a household name.

By 2021, growing demand led to a move across the road into a larger, more modern store. This step was not just about space; it reflected the loyalty of customers and set the foundation for future growth.

The first expansion came in October 2023 with a store in Pizza Takeaway in Willenhall, followed by a successful opening in Pizza Takeaway in Great Barr in March 2024. Between new locations, Aladdin’s strengthened community ties by sponsoring local events, supporting schools, and engaging with the people who had supported the brand since the beginning. Customer-focused initiatives such as Friday pizza deals, exclusive online discounts, and faster, fresher deliveries reinforced its reputation.

By July 2024, Aladdin’s had arrived in Pizza Takeaway in Rugeley, sparking even more excitement. Wolverhampton followed in November with our Pizza Takeaway in Wolverhampton, and December brought a double launch in Pizza Takeaway in Brownhills and Pizza Takeaway in Cannock just in time for the festive season.

Today, Aladdin’s Pizza has become more than just a local takeaway—it’s a community favourite, recognised for handcrafted pizzas, generous portions, and outstanding service. Whether it’s a quick lunch, a family treat, or the perfect movie night feast, Aladdin’s delivers flavour that is hot, fresh, and always satisfying.

The journey continues. Order today and discover why Aladdin’s Pizza is fast becoming the Midlands’ top choice for food takeaway and pizza delivery near me.
